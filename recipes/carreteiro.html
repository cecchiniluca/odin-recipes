Carreteiro Rice

Description

Arroz Carreteiro is meat and rice dish typical from the Minas Gerais state, Brazil. The translation of the name is, Wagoner's Rice. This beef was developed to extend the shelf life. Today it's made with either dried beef, or left over barbecue meat.

Ingredients

  • Leftover barbecue beef diced
  • 1 - 2 cups of rice
  • 2 onions
  • 2 sausage

Steps

  1. Saute onions in 2 tablespoons of olive oil till translucent in color, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the sausage to the pot, breaking it up with a spoon or spatula, and saute till the sausage starts loosing it's pink color. Add cubed beef.
  3. Add the rice.
  4. Stir, cover and cook for 20 - 25 minutes.
